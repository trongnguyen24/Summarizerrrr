### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I set up my API key?**
A: Open the extension settings (gear icon), select your provider, and paste your API key. Keys are stored locally on your device.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. Summarizerrrr stores your keys and settings locally. If you use local models (Ollama), your data never leaves your machine.

**Q: Why isn't the summary generating?**
A: Please check that your API key is correct and that you have sufficient credits/quota with your AI provider.
